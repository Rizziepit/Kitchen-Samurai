Ingredients

200 g sugar beans
3 mutton or pork shanks or 1 ham bone with some meat on it
3 rashers rindless bacon, finely chopped
1.5 litres cold water
30 ml butter or margarine
1 medium onion, finely chopped
2 stalks celery, finely chopped
1 medium carrot, thinly sliced
15 ml salt
2 ml milled pepper

Steps

1 Cover beans with cold water and soak overnight. 
2 Bring the meat, bacon and water to boil in a large saucepan then cover and simmer till the meat is tender.
3 Saute the onion, celery and carrot in butter in a frying pan for 3 minutes.
4 When the meat is tender add the saute'd vegetables and the beans and simmer for 20 to 30 minutes more.
5 Remove the meat and bones, discard the bones and cube the meat.
6 Puree the remains of the saucepan and add the cubed meat.
7 Add salt and pepper, boil and serve.
